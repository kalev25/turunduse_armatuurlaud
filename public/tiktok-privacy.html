NOOR Dashboard Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

NOOR Dashboard is an internal analytics tool used by Ilukliinik NOOR. This policy explains how data from TikTok and other marketing platforms is handled inside the dashboard.

Data We Access

With authorization from a TikTok account administrator, the dashboard may access TikTok account profile information and video performance metrics available through TikTok APIs. The exact data depends on the permissions granted by TikTok and the account administrator.

How Data Is Used

Data is used only for internal marketing analytics, reporting, and performance monitoring for Ilukliinik NOOR. It is not sold or used for unrelated advertising products.

Data Sharing

Dashboard data is available only to authorized users and service providers needed to operate the dashboard, such as Firebase/Google Cloud hosting and database services.

Data Removal

Access can be revoked by disconnecting the TikTok authorization or removing stored API credentials from the dashboard infrastructure.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Ilukliinik NOOR at info@n8r.ee.
